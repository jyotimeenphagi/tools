How to Compress Images Online

Our free image compressor makes it easy to reduce the file size of your photos while maintaining visual quality. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking to browse
  2. Select your desired output format (JPEG, PNG, or WebP)
  3. Adjust the compression level with our quality slider
  4. Optionally resize your image to specific dimensions
  5. Click "Compress Images" and download your optimized files

Why Compress Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster website loading times (improves SEO ranking)
  • Reduced bandwidth usage and server costs
  • Better user experience on mobile devices
  • Meeting platform requirements (social media, email attachments)
